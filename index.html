About ALGo Tools

ALGo Tools is created by Anup Lal Gupta, a software engineer at Meta who is passionate about financial education and helping people achieve financial independence.

Having witnessed the challenges of the rat race firsthand, I believe everyone should understand personal finance to make informed decisions about their future. This calculator is designed to help you:

  • Understand exactly how much money you need for retirement
  • Plan your investments strategically
  • Escape the rat race with proper financial planning
  • Achieve financial independence on your terms

"Financial freedom is not about being rich, it's about having enough to live the life you want without being dependent on a paycheck."
